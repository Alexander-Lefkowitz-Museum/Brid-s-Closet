Welcome, one and all!

We’re excited to inform you that preparations are currently underway for Brid’s Closet’s 10th annual Beltane Spring Festival at the lovely Palaia Vineyards in Highland Mills, NY. This is a momentous occasion for us as it’s the event’s 10th anniversary and we are planning a celebration not to be missed!

The festivities will take place Saturday, May 6, 2017 (no rain date) from 9 A.M. to 10 P.M.

Over 800 guests attended last year’s event and we anticipate even more at this family-friendly, milestone celebration!

 

What’s New for 2017

Along with traditional favorites, we’re adding new features and attractions! The “Mystic Village” is where patrons will find all types of practitioners, from tarot to palm readers, reiki masters and more. There is also the “Best Decorated Booth” contest for our vendors. Patrons will get to vote on which booths have the most festive and attractive decorations - the winner, of course, gets a prize! Furthermore, instead of brochures, we’re designing full-color, 8-page booklets which will include a map of the grounds with activities and vendors’ locations.
